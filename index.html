Our Mission.

Our mission is to build a world where no one’s mental health is hindered by their needs. By building infrastructures to formalize relationships between healthcare and community-based organizations, ItsOhk brings its partners one step closer to closing the mental health equity gaps in their communities. Our Main Goals revolves around :

  • Creating a socio-economic platform which could cater to the mental health issues of the society.
  • Creating a potential network of good counselors all across the country.
  • To reach out to various educational institutes, private institutes, government institutes which would be our potential customers at the first expansion level of the product.
  • To create a competent research team at the backend, which would deal with the common problems faced by institutes. And try to reach out to the common minimum solution.
